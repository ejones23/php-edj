What is the Upper Valley Music Teachers' Association (UVMTA)?

Based in and around Rexburg, Idaho, this music teachers' association has been operating since 1941. Members include full-time, part-time, and student teachers.

What is the UVMTA Junior Music Festival?

Each year we hold Junior Music Festivals and a student Composition Festival. This year's festival will be held on July 14th, 2014 at the BYU-Idaho campus.
